Shakshuka Recipe

Description

A traditional North African and Middle Eastern dish comprised of poached eggs in tomato sauce, peppers, onion, and garlic.

This dish is commonly spiced with parsley, paprika, cayenne pepper, and cumin.


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 chopped onion
  • 3 chopped tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 minced cloves of garlic
    • Seasonings
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Chop onions, tomatoes, mince garlic. Gather rest of ingredients
  2. Heat oil in skillet at medium heat. Stir in onions, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook until vegetables are soft and onion is transparent. Takes about 5-10 minutes.
  3. Mix and stir spices and tomatoes in a bowl.
  4. Stir tomato mix into skillet. Simmer until tomato juices have
  5. Mix indents into tomato mixture and crack eggs into indentations. Cover skills and cook until eggs firm ~5 minutes
  6. Serve and enjoy!
